About Me

My Hobby

I will play Chinese Chess in my free time, as it is my hobby. I started going to the academy when I was 12 years old to learn Chinese Chess tactics. During my secondary school years, I also competed in nearly 100 competitions. I also travelled to China and Taiwan to receive training to improve my Chinese Chess skills. I like Chinese Chess because it can improve my computing skills and logical thinking.

So, Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Francis. In addition to being a student, I have coached Chinese chess in the past. My goal is to assist in the teaching of Chinese chess to all children. I want to make it easier for people to comprehend the idea and the approach employed when playing Chinese chess.

Another thing that I like most is dogs, because they are so cute. In my father's factory, I have about six dogs. I will go to the factory to feed them when I have free time.
