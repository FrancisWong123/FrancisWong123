My Favourite

Spider-Man: No Way Home

Spider-Man: No Way Home is a 2021 American superhero film co-produced by Columbia Pictures and Marvel Studios, and distributed by Sony Pictures Releasing. It is based on the Marvel Comics character Spider-Man.The film was directed by Jon Watts and written by Chris McKenna and Erik Sommers. It stars Tom Holland as Peter Parker alongside Zendaya, Benedict Cumberbatch, Jacob Batalon, Jon Favreau, Jamie Foxx, Willem Dafoe, Alfred Molina, Benedict Wong, Tony Revolori, Marisa Tomei, Andrew Garfield, and Tobey Maguire.
No Way Home premiered at the Fox Village Theatre in Los Angeles on December 13, 2021, and was theatrically released in the United States on December 17 as part of Phase Four of the MCU. The film received positive reviews from critics, who praised the story, direction, action sequences, emotional weight, performances and the cast's chemistry.

Infinite

Infinite is a 2021 American science fiction action film directed by Antoine Fuqua. Written by Ian Shorr, the script is based on a Todd Stein story, which itself was adapted from D. Eric Maikranz's 2009 novel The Reincarnationist Essay. Movie stars Mark Wahlberg, Chiwetel Ejiofor, Sophie Cookson, Jason Manzoukas, Rupert Friend, Toby Jones and Dylan Aube Lane.
The film releases digitally on Paramount+ on June 10, 2021, following a delay in its August 2020 theatrical release due to the COVID-19 pandemic. It received negative reviews from critics who criticized the performance and script, with some comparing it unfavorably to other films like The Matrix.

Raging Fire

Raging Fire is a 2021 Hong Kong, China action film written, produced and directed by Benny Chan in his last directing job before his death on August 23, 2020. The film stars Donnie Yen as a justice cop who meets his former apprentice, Nicholas Xie, who seeks revenge on his former mentor who once put him in jail. The film will be released in mainland China on July 30, 2021, and in Hong Kong on August 19, 2021.
